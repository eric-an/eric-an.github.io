About Me

What's your background?

I received my Bachelor's Degree in Economics from UC San Diego in La Jolla, California. While I was still a junior, I started a family-operated retail business in Irvine, California. After successfully running it for 6 years, I sold the business and participated for a year in California State University Fullerton's Army R.O.T.C. program.

Why start coding?

While I was on summer vacation after a year of volunteering, I decided to try learning coding skills as a hobby. Once I started, I was addicted to the journey - from that initial debilitating feeling of confoundment to the "Hallelujah!" moment of gratification at creating a working product. It is my goal to become a web developer that can implement amazing websites for others and create applications that make people's lives easier.

Why should I hire you?

I have a strong source of self-motivation and diligence to perform well in this web development environment, as it is a career dream I hope to achieve. I possess problem-solving abilities and critical thinking skills that make me an effective web developer. I work hard at continously expanding my knowledge base so that I can perform well individually and on a team.
