About

What's your background?

I received my Bachelor's Degree in Economics from UC San Diego in La Jolla, California. While I was still a junior, I started a family-operated retail business in Irvine, California. After successfully running it for 6 years, I sold the business and participated for a year in California State University Fullerton's Army R.O.T.C. program.

Why start coding?

While I was on summer vacation, I decided to try learning coding skills as a hobby. Once I started, I was addicted to the journey - from that initial debilitating feeling of confoundment to the "Hallelujah!" moment of gratitification.
